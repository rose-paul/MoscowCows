Game Instructions

Use WASD keys to move your character. A large Russian nesting doll will appear on teh screen. The goal is to collect all the dolls in the set while avoiding being trampled my the Moscow Cows. When you collect the doll, its inner doll will appear randomly elsewhere in the field. If you get trampled, you will be notified and you may restart the game using the button on the left.
