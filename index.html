Game Instructions

Player: You are the little green character! Use the WASD keys to make your way through the herd.

Matryoshka: A large Russian nesting doll will appear on the screen. The goal is to collect all the dolls in the set. When you collect, the next inner doll will appear elsewhere on the field. They get small! If you haven't won, look hard for the last doll on the field.

Moscow Cows: Осторожно! While collecting the dolls, be careful of the Moscow Cows coming across the screen! If you get trampled, you will be notified and you may restart the game using the button on the left.

Music: By default, music won't play and cows are silent. However, if you really want to put yourself in the field, go ahead and toggle the sound on (or off) with the button to the right.
